Titas Gas Transmission and Distribution Company Limited (TGTDCL):

Titas Gas Transmission and Distribution Company Limited is a state-owned natural gas company based in Bangladesh. The company is responsible for the transmission and distribution of natural gas throughout the country, serving more than 2.5 million customers. Titas Gas has a vast network of pipelines spanning over 3,600 km, supplying natural gas to households, industries, and commercial entities. The company plays a crucial role in the energy sector of Bangladesh, providing a reliable and affordable source of energy to power the country's economic growth. Titas Gas also places great importance on safety and has implemented measures to ensure the safe operation of its pipelines and facilities.

Job description:
